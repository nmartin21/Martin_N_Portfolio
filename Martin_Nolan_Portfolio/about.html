ABOUT ME

My name is Nolan Martin, I am 21 years old and from Canada. I have been creating art since I was young. I like to create abstract/horror scenes. I have studied CGI and various other areas as a young adult so I can be the best I can be at this profession. Such as lighting, vegatation and anatomy. I can do works such as design, concept art or modeling. For my work, I tend to use Cinema 4D with Photoshop a lot. I think the workflow is fast and great for one person making stuff. Id like to think of myself as a very patient person, so I believe I can do any kind of graphical work as long as my knowledge is in that area.
